#  Superstore Sales Analysis (Excel Data Analyst Project)

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes a retail Superstore dataset to understand business performance, customer behavior, and key factors affecting profitability.

The objective is to extract insights and provide actionable recommendations to improve business performance using Excel-based data analysis.

---

##  Business Problem

The company wants to analyze its historical sales data to:

- Understand product performance  
- Identify regional sales trends  
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior  
- Improve overall profitability  

---
##  Key Insights

- Business shows strong growth; however, profit growth is slower than sales, indicating margin pressure  
- Technology is the most profitable category  
- Furniture generates high sales but low profitability  
- Discounts above 30% consistently lead to losses  
- California, New York, and Washington are top-performing states  
- Consumer segment drives the highest revenue, while Corporate segment has the highest Average Order Value (AOV)  

---

##  Root Cause Analysis

- High discounts significantly reduce profitability  
- Low-margin products combined with heavy discounting lead to losses  
- Inefficient pricing strategy in certain categories (e.g., Furniture)  

---

##  Business Recommendations

- Limit discounts below 30%  
- Promote high-profit categories (Technology, Office Supplies)  
- Optimize pricing strategy for Furniture  
- Reduce discounts on loss-making products  
- Focus on high-performing regions and customer segments
